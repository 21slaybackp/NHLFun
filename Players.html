Positions

In Hockey their are 3 main positions, Forward Defense and Goalie. There are 3 forwards, right wing, left wing and center. The center is the one who takes the faceoffs and the wingers are on his side to help him. The 2 defensive positions are left and right defense. They cover their respected side and prevent goals. Lastly the goalie blocks all the shots that get through.
