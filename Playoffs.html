How It Works

In the NHL the playoffs are 16 teams, 8 per conference and 4 per division. The 1st seed in each division plays the wild card and the 2nd and 3rd seeds face. It is a best of 7 series in which the team with the better regular season record plays in their arena in games 1 2 5 and 7. Whoever gets 4 wins in the series first moves on. There are 4 total rounds and the Stanley Cup Finals rewards the best team in the playoffs.

Finals

The finals is a thrilling series where the best team from the east and the best team of the west faceoff for the title of Stanley Cup Champions and best team in the league. This event happens once a year and tickets are very high demand going for atleast 300$. This year the Capitals beat the Golden Knights and it was really exciting to watch.
