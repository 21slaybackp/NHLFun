History

In 1917 in Montreal, Quebec on November 26 the NHL was created. In its early stages the league only featured six teams. The NHL was created to replace the NHA league because of a corrupt team owner. The NHL stuck to the original six teams until 1967, where on the next page you can learn about the other teams.

Sport

The sport of hockey, specifically ice hockey is played with a puck. In the NHL, there are 3, 20 minute periods in which the teams attempt to score in the opposing teams goal. At the end of the 3rd period Whichever team has the most goals wins the game!
